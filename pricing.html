Payment FAQ

How does payment work?

Card: Visa / Mastercard card checkout opens a hosted RollyPay payment page. After the signed webhook confirms payment, your key appears here and is sent by email.

Telegram Stars: Tap the Image PRO or OMNI Video Stars button, then pay inside @BananaCleanBot.

Crypto: Choose Image PRO 30 days ($2.99), Image PRO yearly ($24.99), or OMNI Video 30 days ($9.99). Plisio opens a hosted invoice page, then the key is issued after the webhook confirms payment.

What are Telegram Stars?

Stars are Telegram's in-app currency. You buy them with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or your bank card — no crypto needed. Image PRO is 250 Stars for 30 days; OMNI Video is 500 Stars for 30 days.

What happens after I pay?

After payment, your license key appears on screen and is sent to your email. Enter it in the extension popup to activate the plan you paid for. Cancel anytime.

I didn't receive my license key email

Your license key is sent to the email you used at checkout (from noreply@banana-clean.app). Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's still missing, contact @BananaCleanBot on Telegram — we'll resend it within a few hours.

What subscription plans are available?

Image PRO: Visa / Mastercard card checkout, 250 Stars, or $2.99 crypto for 30 days; $24.99 yearly.

OMNI Video: Visa / Mastercard card checkout, 500 Stars, or $9.99 crypto for 30 days. This is separate from Image PRO.

Can I use the license key on multiple devices?

Each key activates one browser installation. If you need access on a second device, you will need a second key.

What if I reinstall the browser?

Save your license key. After reinstalling, enter it again in the extension popup and your PRO access will be restored.
