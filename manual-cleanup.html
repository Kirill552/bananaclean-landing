FAQ: removing watermarks and objects from images

How do I remove a watermark from an image online?

Upload the image, set the brush size, paint over the watermark and click Erase painted area. AI rebuilds the background, then the before and after appear side by side.

Is it free and no sign-up?

Yes — the tool is free and needs no account, install or sign-up.

Does the page upload my image?

Cleanup runs in your browser. The original image and mask are sent only after a separate checkbox plus a Send sample click.

Can I remove objects, people, text or logos?

Yes. The brush works on any area: an object, a person in the background, a caption, subtitles, a logo, a signature or a date stamp.

Can I remove the Gemini watermark?

Yes, it works on leftover Gemini and Nano Banana watermarks. To download from Gemini without the mark, the Banana Clean extension is faster.
