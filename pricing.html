Payment FAQ

How does payment work?

Credit/debit card (recommended): Click the Subscribe button on the pricing page. Pay with your credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay via Stripe. You get your license key instantly after payment.

Telegram Stars: Tap "Pay via Telegram", it opens @BananaCleanBot. Pay 250 Stars using Apple Pay, Google Pay, or card — right inside Telegram.

What are Telegram Stars?

Stars are Telegram's in-app currency. You buy them with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or your bank card — no crypto needed. 250 Stars cost approximately $4.99.

What happens after I pay?

After payment, your license key is shown on screen and sent to your email. Enter it in the extension popup to activate PRO. Your access stays active as long as your subscription is running. Cancel anytime.

I didn't receive my license key

Your key is sent to the email you used at checkout (from noreply@banana-clean.app). Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's still missing, contact @BananaCleanBot on Telegram — we'll resend it within a few hours.

What subscription plans are available?

Monthly — $2.99/mo: Full access to all features. Billed monthly, cancel anytime with no penalties.

Yearly — $24.99/yr: Same features, 30% cheaper. Billed once per year ($2.08/mo effective). Cancel anytime.

Both plans activate instantly after payment. Your license key renews automatically.

Can I use the key on multiple devices?

Each key activates one browser installation. If you need access on a second device, you will need a second key.

What if I reinstall the browser?

Save your license key. After reinstalling, enter it again in the extension popup and your PRO access will be restored.
